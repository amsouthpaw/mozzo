Security

The security of your email is very important to us. We analyze your emails only for the purpose of organizing your links, documents and media. We do not store your email. We never see your password. We store anonymous analytics that allow us to provide you with better data and the type of information you actually see when using the application.

Find. Share.

We identify the most meaningful links, documents and media buried within your email. We then present them in an easy to find and share format.

Purposefully discover, or enjoy serendipity.

Photographers, graphic artists and web designers are enjoying the ability to easily find and share media with those who primarily use email. Lawyers, investors and entrepreneurs are enjoying the ability to locate and share their documents. Bloggers, social media professionals and community managers are enjoying the ability to browse for buried and forgotten links.
